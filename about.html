AboutOur hotel

The famous Uzbekistan Hotel has been welcoming visitors for more than three decades and is regarded as one of the emblems of the city.

The hotel now has 243 spacious rooms in various categories. The hotel also has two conference rooms, one large and one smaller, as well as a business center with all the tools required for productive business.
